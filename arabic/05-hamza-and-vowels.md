# Hamza

The sound of the Hamza is a glottal stop and is transliterated as <ins>'</ins>.

<hr>

### _<ins>Exmaples of Hamza:</ins>_

| أب |      إن |   رئيس  | شؤون   | شاطئ | شهداء       |
--------|------|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| 'ab   | 'énn | ra'iis | shu'uun | shaati' | shuhada' |
